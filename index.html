Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is a 2 year old live service game developed by miHoYo Co., Ltd. (AKA HoYoverse under subsidiary Cognosphere Pte., Ltd.). The company sought to replicate the success of its former games, Honkai Impact 3rd, Zenless Zone Zero and Tears of Themis. As of November 2022, the game has won multiple awards, boasts a player base of approximately 64 million users, and has grossed more than $4.5 billion in revenue.

NieR: Automata

NieR:Automata is a 2017 action role-playing game developed by PlatinumGames and published by Square Enix. The game is a sequel to the 2010 game Nier, a spinoff of Drakengard. As of the October release of the game's Nintendo Switch port, The End of YoRHa Edition, the game has surpassed 7 million units in sales, received multiple accolades, and staked its claim in gaming history with multiple collaborations.

Persona 5 Royal

Persona 5 Royal is the 2019 enhanced version of Persona 5, a 2016 role-playing video game developed by Atlus. It is the sixth installment in the Persona series, and the 47th release in the larger Megami Tensei franchise. Being the best selling MegaTen game with over 5 million worldwide copies sold, the game has been praised by critics and fans alike, and been cited as one of the greatest role-playing video games of all time.

Danganronpa: Trigger Happy Havoc

Danganronpa: Trigger Happy Havoc is a 2010 visual novel adventure game published by Spike Chunsoft. The game's dark, controversial content and innovative gameplay have made it both a cult classic and commercial success. The game has recieved 2 manga adaptations, an anime adapation, a sequel and multiple spinoff games, and as of October 2021, has mangaged to sell over 5 million copies across the entire franchise, with 3 million units sold on PC alone.
