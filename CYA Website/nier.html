Game Storyline

Nier Automata is a 26 ending JRPG taking place in a distant future, on an earth overrun with machines. You play as YoRHa squad member 2B sent on a mission to clear out the earth of machines to allow mankind to return home from the moon. This thrilling game fully immerses you in the story and refuses to let you go as you develop relationships through side quests and the main story. The combat in the game allows you to feel like a super solider with the right balance of complexity and fairness.

Trivia

  • 2B is a featured fighter in Soulcalibur VI.

  • 2B's name is a Shakesphere refrence from the famous Hamlet monolouge.

  • In game you can have 2B pet Pod 042.

  • NieR: Automata is a direct sequel to the first game of the series, NieR.

  • The game is hack and slash style, meaning the games combat is primarily melee based.

  • It is an open world game, encouraging players to explore and interact with the enviornment rather than focus only on the story.
