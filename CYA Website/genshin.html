Game Storyline

Genshin Impact is a live service anime RPG set in the mysterious world of Teyvat. You play as a traveler new to this world looking for their twin lost in a catastrophic event. Accompanied by Paimon, the best guide around (and affectionately referred to as "emergency food"), you traverse through 7 regions, each represented by a different element, making friends along the way and protecting the world from the mysterious Abyss Order. In the game you have five weapon classes; sword, bow, catalyst, polearm and claymore. With four slots character slots per team, you can design your layout to your playstyle in order to explore and get stronger while becoming further immersed in the story.
