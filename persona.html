Game Storyline

Persona 5 Royal is an expanded upon JRPG, stemming from the widely beloved and critically acclaimed game, Persona 5. Set in modern day Japan you play as Joker, a teenage delinquent looking to fit into his new enviornment. While fixing the wrongs of society with your friends, you must maintain your cover as a normal high school student. While doing so you can develop closer bonds with the people you come across, hold a part time job, put in extra hours after school and even venture deeper into Japan if you choose.

Trivia

  • The phantom theives personas are based off of real or fictional criminals.

  • In the subway station, you can find posters with refrences to Persona 4.

  • The design of the tarot deck used in game was based off a popular tarot deck in 16th century France.

  • Shujin Academy (the school in game) translates to Prison Academy.

  • Dr. Maruki is voiced by Billy Kametz, who unfortunatly passed earlier in 2022. You can hear him as Ferdinand Von Aegir in noteable Fire Emblem titles and Josuke Higashikata in the dub of Jojo's Bizzare Adventure: Diamond is Unbreakable.
