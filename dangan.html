Game Storyline

Danganronpa Trigger Happy Havoc is a japanense murder mystery visual novel following a class of 16 high school students in Hopes Peak Academy; a school designed for the best of the best in every talent. When they're forced to play a death game with the headmaster Monokuma, it's up to them to uncover whos behind each murder in order to survive.

Trivia

  • There is a secret alternate ending to the game.

  • The first two victims of the killing game were the least popular charecters for the development team.

  • There are 4 games with the name Danganronpa in the series, only 3 follow the main story. Those games are; Trigger Happy Havoc, Danganronpa 2: Goodbye Despair, and Ultimate Despair Girls.

  • Danganronpa V3: Killing Harmony is the most debated game of the franchise, with fans asking if it's canon.

  • So far the franchise has sold over 5 million copies as reported in 2021.

  • Danganronpa THH is 10 years old, with Spike Chunsoft releasing a class photo with every Danganronpa student sporting a formal outfit.
